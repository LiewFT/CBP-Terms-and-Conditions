Our story

Changi Bay Polytechnic is a leading educational institution that

has been shaping the minds of future leaders for over 11 years.

Founded in 2015, our polytechnic has a rich history of academic excellence and innovation.

We are committed to providing a nurturing environment

that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to ensure that our students receive

the highest quality education and are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the future.

Our founder Sha Sha Shamugan envisoned a school where students could have a well rounded education.

Our Vision

We hope our students will carry our motto "Forgiving the past, Forging the future" with them throughout their lives.
